Without Rankuzo
  • You struggle with tools like Excel to enter contestants and points and rank them by total result.
  • You reveal the final ranking and results of contestants in an unattractive way.
With Rankuzo
  • You just enter the names of contestants and points - Rankuzo does all the math.
  • You reveal the final results and ranking of contestants in an attractive way.
  • A tense atmosphere is created during the announcement of the winners.
  • The winners can proudly take a picture in front of the screen showing their result and share it on social media.
